Lauren C. Williams
Reporter. Storyteller. Tech enthusiast and culture maven in Washington, D.C.
I became a journalist to tell stories and learn more about the world around me. Throughout my career, I've writtern about the social impact of technology and the humans that build it, health care, politics, pop culture, and crime. I love long-form reporting, thoughtful commentary, and experimenting with different media. My work has appeared in multiple outlets including ThinkProgress, The Week, The Seattle Times, ElevationDC, The Washington Post, The Washington Examiner, and Fox News.
