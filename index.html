Lauren C. Williams is currently the tech reporter for ThinkProgress, where she frequently writes about the social impact of technology and the humans that build it. Before writing about tech, Lauren reported on health care, politics, and crime. She enjoys long-form storytelling and experimenting with different media. Throughout her career Lauren’s work has also appeared in The Week, The Seattle Times, ElevationDC, The Washington Post, The Washington Examiner, and Fox News. She has a bachelor’s in dietetics from the University of Delaware and a master’s in journalism from the University
of Maryland, College Park.
